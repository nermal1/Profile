Residency

I was born and raised in Algonquin Illinois. I lived there for 20 years of my life. It was a great place not too far from the city and not too far from the country. My favorite part about Algonquin is the Fox River. It is a great fishing spot especially for smallmouth bass. If you or anyone you know were to go there I would recommend going to Riverbottom Ice Cream. They are local business and it tastes great.

I recently moved to Belvidere Illinois this summer. It is just to the east of Rockford so there is plenty to do. It is a much older looking town but once you get to know the place it is pretty nice. The roads are very fun to drive as most of them are just cruising through the countryside. I would reccomend going to the Apollo Theatre if there is a concert when you are there. It is a great music venue and right outside is the Kishwaukee river.

I now am living in Carbondale just off the Southern Illinois Campus. I came to Carbondale because of all the great things I have heard about southern Illinois. From my short stay so far I can say this place lives up to expectations and some. The nature here is amazing, it doesn't even feel like I am in Illinois anymore. The mountains and trails are very beautiful. There is so much to explore here that I am sure you can always find something to do.
